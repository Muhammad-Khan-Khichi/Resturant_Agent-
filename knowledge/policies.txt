OPENING HOURS:
Restaurant is open from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily.

ORDER POLICY:
- Orders can be modified before checkout.
- Once checkout is complete, order cannot be changed.

RESERVATION POLICY:
- Reservations recommended on weekends.
- Table held for 15 minutes only after reservation time.

PAYMENT POLICY:
- Payment is collected at checkout stage.
- Cash and card payments supported (simulation in system).

REFUND POLICY:
- Refunds only applicable before order preparation starts.

DELIVERY POLICY:
- Delivery available within 5 km radius.
- Delivery time: 30–45 minutes depending on load.

FOOD SAFETY:
- All meat is halal certified.
- Food is prepared fresh per order.

CANCELLATION:
- Orders can be cancelled before checkout only.
